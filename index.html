Techlon 2013
Techelon is an annual science and technology festival hosted by Sir Chhotu Ram Institute of Engineering and Technology, C.C.S. University, Meerut. Started with the aim of providing a platform for students to develop and showcase their technical prowess, it has now become an intercollege affair and is one of its kind. At techelon, we aim at encouraging the young talents to exhibit and exchange their innovative ideas and skills in the field of science and technology through various activities like engineering quiz, robo gaming, technical PPT, group discussions, working model presentation, to name a few. Techelon also features a varied range of fun events that might make you experience a euphoric high. All in all, it not only gives a huge amount of experience but also serves as a pedestal to the path of glory by enabling the students to set a benchmark in the engineering revolution.

Scriet Cultural Society
Sir Chhotu Ram Institute of Engineering and Technology’s cultural society, popularly known as SCS, is a student body that is responsible for organizing all the events happening in the college round the year, including the annual technical fest ‘Techelon’ and the annual charity ‘Aashayein’. SCS aims at providing a platform to the students to exhibit their talents and creativity in varied fields, ensuring an overall enhancement of their personalities.
SCS comprises of six clubs to take care of the varied fields of events.
